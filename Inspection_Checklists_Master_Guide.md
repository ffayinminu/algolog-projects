## ğŸ“Š SCORING GUIDELINES:

### **STRICT vs LENIENT SCORING:**

**STRICT (Recommended for Liability Protection):**
- All checkpoints must pass for full points
- Partial compliance = proportional points
- Example: 5 out of 6 checkpoints = 83% of points

**LENIENT (For Encouraging Improvement):**
- Focus on major issues
- Give benefit of doubt on minor items
- Example: 5 out of 6 checkpoints = 90% of points

**RECOMMENDED:** Start STRICT, explain scoring clearly to client, adjust if needed for relationship building.
