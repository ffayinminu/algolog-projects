# Kanti Plus - Supermarket Revenue, Checkout & Retention System

## Overview
A comprehensive revenue growth, faster checkout, and customer retention strategy for Kanti Plus Supermarket in Kaduna. Integrates a wallet-based checkout system, tiered loyalty program, smart POS dashboard, inventory intelligence, and peak hours management.

## Client
**Kanti Plus Supermarket** — Kaduna, Nigeria

## Problem Addressed
- Long checkout queues during peak hours (avg 2:47 per transaction)
- 3 in 10 customers leave the queue (queue abandonment)
- Cash-only transactions = slow counting
- No customer retention/loyalty program
- Manual inventory leading to ~NGN 400K/month in expired stock waste
- No data on sales patterns or product performance
- Estimated NGN 1.25M+ monthly revenue at risk

## Solutions

### 1. Customer Wallet System
- One-tap checkout via pre-loaded wallet (phone number based)
- Fund via bank transfer, cash, or card
- Transaction time: ~28 seconds (down from 2:47)
- 150% more checkout throughput

### 2. Loyalty Packages & Rewards (3 Tiers)
| Tier | Points | Discounts | Perks |
|------|--------|-----------|-------|
| Silver | 1 pt per NGN 100 | 5% on select items | Birthday reward: NGN 500 credit |
| Gold | 2 pts per NGN 100 | 10% on select items | Monthly NGN 1,000 credit, priority checkout |
| Premium | 3 pts per NGN 100 | 15% storewide | Quarterly gift basket, VIP counter, pre-order access |

Projected: 73% of loyalty customers shop weekly, 40% higher basket size, 85% retention at 6 months.

### 3. Smart POS Dashboard & Inventory Intelligence
- Real-time revenue, transactions, avg basket size, active loyalty members
- Sales by hour charts (identify peak periods)
- Payment method breakdown
- Inventory alerts: low stock reorder, expiry warnings with promo suggestions
- Staff performance tracking
- Stock waste reduced from NGN 400K/month to NGN 60K/month

### 4. Peak Hours Strategy
- Dynamic counter allocation based on time/demand
- Express lane for wallet/loyalty customers (15 items or less)
- Real-time queue monitoring with mobile alerts
- Pre-planned staffing + weekend promotional events (2x loyalty points)

## Implementation Roadmap (6 Weeks)
| Week | Phase | Activities |
|------|-------|-----------|
| 1 | Discovery & Audit | Store layout, POS review, peak hour analysis, network check |
| 2-3 | Hardware & Infrastructure | POS terminals, barcode scanners, network, power backup (after hours) |
| 3-4 | Software Deployment | Wallet system, loyalty engine, POS dashboard, inventory module (sandbox testing) |
| 4-5 | Staff Training | Cashier training, manager training, quick-ref cards, support line |
| 5-6 | Launch | Soft launch, full go-live, on-site support, weekly reviews |

All installation happens after trading hours. Store never closes during implementation.

## Projected Results
- 150% more checkout throughput
- NGN 1.25M/month revenue recovered
- 85% customer retention at 6 months
- NGN 340K/month saved on inventory
- 28-second average checkout
- 6-week deployment timeline
