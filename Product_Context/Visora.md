# Visora - AI-Powered Data Analytics & Insights

## Overview
Visora is built to help organizations harness the power of data. It connects to business data sources and provides AI-driven insights, custom dashboards, and decision support.

Note: Visora has also been described in some contexts as a travel & booking platform. The product scope may have evolved — confirm current direction with the team.

## Core Features
- **Unified Data Visualization** — Connects to business data sources (finance, CRM, operations)
- **AI-Driven Insights** — Identifies patterns and forecasts trends
- **Custom Dashboards** — Tailored views for executives, managers, and teams
- **Performance Monitoring** — Real-time KPIs across departments
- **Decision Support** — Recommendation engine highlights profit opportunities or inefficiencies
- **Document Search & Collaboration** — Project-based document management with AI analysis
- **Q&A on Documents** — Users can ask questions about uploaded documents

## Target Markets
- Enterprises needing data-driven decision making
- Organizations with scattered data across multiple systems
- Management teams needing real-time operational visibility

## Value Proposition
Visora makes businesses data-driven, enabling smarter decisions that directly impact revenue and growth.
