# Focal Point Tracer - Garment Traceability System

## Overview
A fast-deployment garment traceability solution for dry cleaning facilities. Eliminates garment loss and reduces order assembly time using QR-coded heat seal labels and a tablet-based tracking application.

## Client
**Focal Point Drycleaners** — Abuja-based dry cleaning business

## Problem Solved
- Paper tags detach during cleaning, making garments untraceable
- Staff waste hours searching for missing pieces
- Lost items become "orphans" at end of day
- No visibility into which items are at which station

## Key Features
- **Durable QR-Coded Heat Seals** — Permanent, wash-proof heat seal labels with unique QR codes as digital fingerprints
- **Tablet-Based Tracer App** with three core functions:
  - **Check-in** — Link garments to orders by printing/applying unique QR code
  - **Station Scan** — 2-second scan updates garment location at each station (Washing, Pressing, etc.)
  - **Assembly** — Instant visibility of all items in an order, eliminating manual searches
