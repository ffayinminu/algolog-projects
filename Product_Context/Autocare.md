# Autocare - Service Reminder System

## Overview
A centralized platform designed to help automotive businesses automatically notify their customers when their vehicles are due for servicing. Built on Airtable + Zapier + SyncMate stack.

## Core Features
- **Customer Management** — Register and manage customers per business
- **Vehicle Registry** — Track vehicles and their service history
- **Automated Service Reminders** — Email & WhatsApp reminders at 7 days and 3 days before due date
- **Manual Messaging** — Send custom messages to customers
- **Activity Logging** — All automated actions logged for transparency
- **Multi-Tenant Architecture** — Each business (auto-mechanic) sees only their own data
- **Personalized Dashboards** — Clean interface for each business via Airtable Interfaces

## How It Works
1. Algolog creates an Airtable account for the business
2. Business receives an Interface-only access link (no backend visibility)
3. User manages customers, vehicles, services, and messages from their dashboard
4. System automatically calculates next service dates
5. Zapier sends automated reminders via email/WhatsApp
6. All actions logged in Tenant Activity Log

## Tech Stack
- **Airtable** (Paid Plan) — Core database and dashboard
- **Zapier** (Paid Plan) — Automation engine for reminders and notifications
- **SyncMate** — WhatsApp integration (no external branding, no limitations)

## Target Market
- Auto-mechanic workshops
- Car service centers
- Fleet management companies
- Any automotive business wanting automated customer follow-up

## Value Proposition
Makes customer follow-up automatic, efficient, and reliable so auto-mechanics can increase service retention and improve customer experience.

## Status
Built and operational (demo-ready).
