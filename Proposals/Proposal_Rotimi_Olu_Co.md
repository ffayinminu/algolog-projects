## **1. Executive Summary**

Rotimi Olu & Co, founded in 2012 and registered with NIESV and ESVARBON, has established itself as a professional estate surveying and valuation firm in Abuja. To enhance operational efficiency, expand client reach, and strengthen professional service delivery, **Algolog Limited** proposes a strategic technology partnership focused on digital transformation.

Our approach will enable Rotimi Olu & Co to:

- Streamline valuation and surveying workflows
- Enhance client relationship management
- Improve report generation and delivery
- Build a stronger digital presence and professional brand
- Create data-driven processes for market insights
