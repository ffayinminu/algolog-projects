- Identify key gaps and opportunities in CRM, listings, logistics, and marketing

### **Phase 2: Technology Integration & Platform Development (Weeks 3–8)**

**Activities:**

- Develop a custom property management portal with listings, dashboards, and booking systems

**Deliverables:**

- Fully functional property portal
- CRM system integrated with sales workflow
- AI tools for customer engagement
- Analytics dashboards

**KPIs:**

- 30% increase in online leads
- 24/7 customer query response capability

- 20% reduction in project delays
