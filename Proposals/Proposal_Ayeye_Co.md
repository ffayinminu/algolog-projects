## **1. Executive Summary**

Ayeye & Co Estate Surveyors & Valuers, founded in 2009 under the leadership of Victor Ayeye, has grown to 37 employees with $7.3M in revenue and an impressive client roster including First Bank, Access Bank, Stanbic IBTC, and AMCON. To maintain industry leadership and scale operations, **Algolog Limited** proposes a comprehensive digital transformation partnership.

Our approach will enable Ayeye & Co to:

- Streamline valuation and surveying operations for enterprise clients
- Enhance relationship management with financial institution clients
- Improve report quality and delivery speed
- Build on existing market leadership with technology advantage
- Create data-driven insights for property valuations
