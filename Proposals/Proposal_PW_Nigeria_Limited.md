## **1. Executive Summary**

PW Nigeria Limited has built a remarkable legacy spanning over 45 years in Nigeria's construction industry. As part of the BUA International group, PW Nigeria is well-positioned for continued growth. To further enhance operational efficiency, project delivery, and competitive positioning, **Algolog Limited** proposes a strategic technology partnership.

Our approach will enable PW Nigeria Limited to:

- Optimize project management and reduce construction delays through AI-powered tracking
- Streamline procurement and supply chain operations
- Enhance workforce productivity and resource allocation
- Gain real-time visibility across all active projects
- Strengthen client communication and stakeholder reporting
