## **1. Executive Summary**

Arab Contractors Nigeria Limited, part of a global construction powerhouse with over 60,000 employees and operations across 29 countries, brings unparalleled expertise to major infrastructure projects in Nigeria. To further enhance local operational efficiency while maintaining alignment with global standards, **Algolog Limited** proposes a technology partnership focused on digital transformation.

Our approach will enable Arab Contractors Nigeria to:

- Streamline large-scale project management with AI-powered tracking
- Optimize resource allocation across multiple concurrent projects
- Enhance coordination between local teams and regional offices
- Improve procurement efficiency and supply chain visibility
- Strengthen client reporting and stakeholder communication
