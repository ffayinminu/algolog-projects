Dantata & Sawoe Construction Company stands as one of Nigeria's most established indigenous construction firms, with over 40 years of experience, 3,000+ employees, and a track record of delivering major infrastructure projects. To continue leading in an increasingly competitive market, **Algolog Limited** proposes a comprehensive digital transformation partnership.

Our approach will enable Dantata & Sawoe to:

- Modernize project management across all active construction sites

- Streamline procurement and vendor management processes
- Enhance financial visibility and cost control
- Build competitive advantage through technology leadership
