## **1. Executive Summary**

SABC Limited (Sazak Acres Biloque Company), founded in 2007, has grown into a full-service construction company with 201-500 employees delivering projects across Nigeria. To continue scaling operations while maintaining quality and efficiency, **Algolog Limited** proposes a comprehensive digital transformation partnership.

Our approach will enable SABC Limited to:

- Optimize project management across all construction sites
- Enhance coordination among the 200+ employee workforce
- Streamline procurement and supply chain operations
- Improve client communication and project transparency
- Build a data-driven culture for continuous improvement
