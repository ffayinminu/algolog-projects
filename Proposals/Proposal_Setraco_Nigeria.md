## **1. Executive Summary**

Setraco Nigeria Limited, incorporated in 1977 with over 35 years of experience and part of the SETRACO GROUP (30+ companies, $1B+ turnover), is a leader in Nigeria's construction industry. To further strengthen operational excellence and maintain industry leadership, **Algolog Limited** proposes an enterprise-scale digital transformation partnership.

Our approach will enable Setraco Nigeria to:

- Implement world-class project management systems across all operations
- Optimize resource allocation and equipment utilization
- Enhance coordination across the SETRACO GROUP network
- Strengthen quality control and safety compliance
- Build predictive analytics for strategic planning
