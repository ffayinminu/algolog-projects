# Spacer Lead Magnet Scorecard

**Product:** Spacer — Cloud-based shortlet & hotel management platform (spacer.so)

**Date:** March 2, 2026

---

## Scoring Framework

Each lead magnet is scored across 12 indices derived from Hormozi's 7-Step Lead Magnet framework. Scale: 1-5 per index (60 max). Minimum viable: 45/60. A score of 1 on indices 1-4 = do not build.

| Category | Indices |
|----------|---------|
| Quality Gates | 1. Engages Ideal Customers on Sight — 2. Engagement Lift vs Core Offer — 3. Consumption Completeness — 4. Purchase Intent Lift |
| Quality Dimensions | 5. Problem Narrowness & Reveal — 6. Solution-Delivery Fit — 7. Headline-ability — 8. Consumption Ease — 9. Value Depth — 10. CTA Bridge |
| Economics & Scale | 11. Cost Efficiency — 12. Scalability |
