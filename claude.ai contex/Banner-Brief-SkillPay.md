### 2. The Problem
Building payment infrastructure from scratch for each new product is expensive, slow, and error-prone. Nigerian businesses need reliable payment processing that handles Paystack integration, merchant accounts, custom links, and bank settlements — without rebuilding it every time a new product launches.

### 3. Key Value Props
1. **Multi-Brand Support** — One backend powers unlimited frontend products under different brands
2. **Battle-Tested** — Already serving 4 live product domains in production
3. **Full Payment Stack** — Payments, accounts, links, settlements, and recurring billing in one system
4. **Nigerian-First** — Built specifically for Paystack, Nigerian banks, and local compliance requirements

### 4. How It Works
1. Connect your product frontend to SkillPay's API
2. SkillPay handles payment processing, user accounts, and custom link generation
3. Customers pay through your branded experience without ever seeing the SkillPay layer
4. Funds settle to merchant bank accounts automatically

### 5. Case Study
SkillPay currently powers 4 live product domains: skillpay.link, buymeshawarma.com, buymeabeverage.com, and autogiving.ng (Pledged). Each product has its own brand, audience, and payment flow, all running on the same backend infrastructure.

## BACK SIDE

### 1. Feature Breakdown
1. **Multi-Brand/Tenant Architecture** — One backend serves multiple products, each with its own branding and domain
2. **Paystack Integration** — Deep integration with Paystack for card payments, bank transfers, and mobile money
3. **User/Merchant Accounts** — Full account management system for both payers and recipients
4. **Custom Link Generation** — Unique payment links for each merchant (e.g., domain.com/[merchantname])
5. **Transaction Logging** — Complete audit trail for every payment, settlement, and account action
6. **Bank Payout** — Automatic settlement to Nigerian bank accounts via Paystack
7. **Recurring Subscriptions** — charge_authorization support for automated recurring payments
8. **Paystack Subaccount Support** — Direct merchant settlement without funds touching the platform
