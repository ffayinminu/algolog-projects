### 2. The Problem
Auto-mechanic workshops lose repeat customers because they have no system for follow-up. Customers forget when their next service is due, and mechanics have no organized way to track vehicles, service history, or send timely reminders. The result is lost revenue and weakened customer relationships.

### 3. Key Value Props
1. **Automatic Reminders** — WhatsApp and email alerts sent at 7 days and 3 days before service is due
2. **Customer Retention** — Customers come back on time, every time, without the workshop having to chase them
3. **Zero App Download** — Runs on a clean Airtable interface, no installation or technical setup needed
4. **Complete History** — Every vehicle, every service, every date tracked in one organized system

### 4. How It Works
1. Workshop receives their personalized AutoCare dashboard
2. Register customers and vehicles with service details and mileage
3. System calculates next service dates automatically based on intervals
4. Automated WhatsApp and email reminders are sent before each service is due

## BACK SIDE

### 1. Feature Breakdown
1. **Customer Management** — Full customer profiles with contact details, vehicle information, and service preferences
2. **Vehicle Registry** — Track multiple vehicles per customer with make, model, year, and mileage records
3. **Automated Service Reminders** — WhatsApp and email reminders sent automatically at 7-day and 3-day intervals before service is due
4. **Manual Messaging** — Send custom messages to individual customers for special offers or updates
5. **Activity Logging** — Complete log of all interactions, services performed, and communications sent
6. **Multi-Tenant Architecture** — Each workshop gets their own isolated, personalized dashboard
7. **Personalized Dashboards** — Clean, branded interface customized for each workshop's needs
8. **Automatic Next-Service Calculation** — System calculates the next service date based on service type and recommended intervals
