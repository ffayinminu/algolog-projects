### 2. The Problem
Churches lose significant giving income when members travel, relocate, or simply forget. Manual cash collection lacks transparency and creates reconciliation headaches. Sporadic giving makes it nearly impossible to plan budgets or fund ongoing programs with any confidence.

### 3. Key Value Props
1. **Giver-Initiated Adoption** — Members start using it independently, no church-wide rollout needed
2. **Direct Settlement** — Funds go straight to the church's bank account, never touch Algolog
3. **Zero Lock-In** — Members pause, adjust, or cancel anytime with full control
4. **Predictable Income** — Converts sporadic giving to reliable recurring revenue
5. **Full Transparency** — Every transaction tracked, auditable, with digital receipts for both church and giver

### 4. How It Works
1. Church signs up and sets their bank details for direct settlement
2. Church customizes their branded giving page with logo, colors, and giving categories
3. Members visit autogiving.ng/[church-name], pick a category, set their amount and frequency
4. System automatically charges on schedule, sends receipts, and settles directly to the church

### 5. Case Study
181+ churches targeted across Abuja with active outreach campaign and lead magnets. 84 additional churches targeted nationwide. Campaign includes personalized proposals and follow-up cadence.

## BACK SIDE

### 1. Feature Breakdown
1. **Church-Branded Giving Links** — Every church gets their own custom URL (autogiving.ng/[church-name]) with branded colors and logo
2. **Recurring Automation** — Weekly, monthly, or custom frequency giving schedules that run automatically
3. **One-Time Giving** — Supporters can give once without creating an account or signing up
4. **Direct Paystack Subaccount Settlement** — Funds settle directly to the church's bank via Paystack subaccount, not through Algolog
5. **Member Self-Service Dashboard** — Givers manage their own giving: view history, adjust amounts, pause, or cancel
6. **Church Admin Dashboard** — Full oversight with transaction reports, member activity, and CSV export for accounting
7. **Multi-Church Platform Accounts** — Denominations or church networks can manage multiple churches from one account
8. **Automated Receipts** — Email and SMS receipts sent automatically after every transaction
