# INDUSTRY 1: HOSPITALITY
*Hotels, Serviced Apartments, Short-Lets, Resorts*

### 1. GuestPilot — "Your Property Runs Itself While You Sleep"
An autopilot booking engine. Guests find you, book, pay, get a smart lock code, check in, and check out — all without you touching your phone. Cart abandonment recovery chases guests who almost booked. You get your evenings back.

### 2. KeyGhost — "No More Keys. No More Front Desk. No More Midnight Drives."
TTLock smart lock integration that generates unique access codes per booking. Code activates on check-in day, expires at checkout. Guest gets it by SMS. No physical key handover ever again. Remote lock/unlock from your dashboard.

### 3. BookRecover — "Recover 15-25% of Bookings That Would Have Disappeared"
A standalone cart abandonment system. When a guest starts booking but doesn't complete payment, the system sends 3 automatic reminders — at 30 minutes, 2 hours, and 24 hours — with a direct link to finish. Recovers revenue you never knew you were losing.

### 4. PropertySite — "Your Property's Own Website — Live in 2 Weeks"
An auto-generated luxury marketing website for your property. Hero images, room showcase, pricing, booking button, location map, and contact details. Mobile responsive. SEO optimised so you appear when people search for properties in your area. Not a template — your brand.

### 5. RoomPulse — "Know Your Occupancy, Revenue, and Trends — In Real Time"
A revenue and occupancy analytics dashboard. See your occupancy rate, average daily rate, revenue per available room, booking sources, seasonal patterns, and forecasts. Know which rooms book most, which sit empty, and whether you're pricing right — all from your phone.

### 6. GuestTalk — "5-Star Guest Communication Without Typing a Single Message"
Automated guest messaging from pre-arrival to post-checkout. Pre-arrival instructions with access code and directions. Welcome message on check-in day. Mid-stay check-in. Checkout reminder. Post-stay review request. Every guest gets the same premium experience. Zero manual effort.

### 7. TurnoverFlow — "Every Checkout Triggers a Clean. Every Clean Returns the Room."
A housekeeping workflow engine that connects to your booking system. Guest checks out → room flagged for cleaning → cleaner notified → cleaner marks ready → room re-enters booking pool. No room ever sits empty because someone forgot to flag it as clean.

### 8. FlexRate — "Charge the Right Price Every Night — Automatically"
A dynamic pricing engine that adjusts rates based on day of week, season, occupancy level, and demand. Weekends at 90% occupancy? Price goes up. Midweek at 20%? Discounted rate fills the room. You set the rules. The system adjusts automatically. Every empty room is money lost.

### 9. GuestLoop — "Turn One-Time Visitors Into Lifetime Guests"
A guest CRM and loyalty platform. Captures every guest's details, tracks their stays, sends personalised return offers, birthday messages, and "we miss you" re-engagement. Referral program: "Refer a friend, both get NGN 5,000 off." Builds the repeat business that Airbnb can't.

### 10. EventBook — "Your Conference Room Earns Money While Your Rooms Sleep"
A dedicated booking system for conference rooms, event halls, and meeting spaces. Hourly and daily rates. Online calendar showing real-time availability. Online payment. Add-ons (projector, catering). Companies find and book your space without calling. Revenue from an asset that currently sits empty 5 days a week.
