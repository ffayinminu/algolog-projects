### 2. The Problem
Businesses and individuals need to transfer sensitive files and financial data, but standard channels lack encryption and meaningful privacy guarantees. Across Africa, data breaches and interception risks continue to grow, and the tools available were not designed with this level of security in mind.

### 3. Key Value Props
1. **End-to-End Encryption** — Every transfer is protected from sender to receiver, with no exposure along the way
2. **Privacy by Design** — No data is stored longer than necessary, reducing risk at every stage
3. **Built for African Businesses** — Designed to meet local compliance requirements and work reliably on African infrastructure

### 4. How It Works
1. Sign up and verify your identity
2. Upload your file or initiate your transfer
3. Recipient receives a secure access link
4. Transfer completes with full encryption, then data is automatically cleared

## BACK SIDE

### 1. Feature Breakdown
1. **End-to-End Encrypted Transfers** — Military-grade encryption protects every file and transaction from sender to receiver
2. **Secure File Sharing** — Share documents, spreadsheets, and media files with confidence that only the intended recipient can access them
3. **Private Financial Transactions** — Transfer financial data with full encryption and no third-party exposure
4. **Identity Verification** — Both sender and recipient are verified before transfer proceeds
5. **Full Audit Trail** — Every transfer is logged with timestamps, status, and access records for compliance
6. **Granular Access Controls** — Set who can view, download, or forward transferred content
7. **Expiring Links** — Transfer links expire after a set time or number of downloads, preventing unauthorized future access
8. **Mobile-Responsive** — Works seamlessly on desktop and mobile devices across all major browsers
