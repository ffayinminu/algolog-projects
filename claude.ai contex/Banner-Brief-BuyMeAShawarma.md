### 2. The Problem
Content creators, freelancers, and professionals in Nigeria need a simple, culturally resonant way to accept casual tips and gifts from supporters. Shawarma is one of Nigeria's most popular street foods, making this a fun and relatable way to support the people who create value in your life.

### 3. Key Value Props
1. **Culturally Relevant** — Shawarma is Nigeria's favorite, making gifting feel fun and familiar
2. **Custom Shareable Link** — Your own buymeshawarma.com/[yourname] link, easy to remember and share
3. **Instant Bank Settlement** — Funds go directly to your Nigerian bank account with no delays or intermediaries

### 4. How It Works
1. Create your free profile on buymeshawarma.com
2. Get your custom shareable link
3. Share it everywhere — social media, WhatsApp, bio links, email signatures
4. Fans buy you shawarma, and the money goes straight to your bank account
