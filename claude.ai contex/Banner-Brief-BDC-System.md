### 2. The Problem
Bureau De Change operators run their businesses through WhatsApp messages and phone calls. There is no structured workflow, no audit trail, and customers cannot check rates without calling. This disorganization leads to settlement delays, errors, and a lack of operational visibility that makes growth difficult.

### 3. Key Value Props
1. **Public Rate Display** — Customers check live USD/GBP rates without needing to call
2. **Digital Receipt Upload** — Customers submit receipts directly through the platform, no more WhatsApp photos
3. **Structured Workflow** — Every transaction follows a clear pipeline from receipt to settlement
4. **Owner Analytics** — Full dashboard showing operations, volumes, and performance at a glance

### 4. How It Works
1. BDC owner sets live FX rates and lists domiciliary accounts on the platform
2. Customer views rates online and uploads their transfer receipt
3. Back-office staff processes each transaction through a structured pipeline
4. Owner monitors everything from the analytics dashboard with full visibility

## BACK SIDE

### 1. Feature Breakdown
1. **Public FX Rate Display** — Live rates published online so customers can check USD, GBP, and other currencies without calling
2. **Receipt Upload** — Customers submit transfer receipts (image or PDF) directly through the platform
3. **Domiciliary Account Listing** — BDC owner lists all available domiciliary accounts for customer transfers
4. **Transaction Workflow Pipeline** — Every transaction moves through defined stages: received, verified, processing, settled
5. **Staff Assignment and Management** — Assign transactions to specific back-office staff with role-based access
6. **Status Notifications** — Customers and staff receive updates at each stage of the transaction pipeline
7. **Analytics Dashboard** — Owner-level view of transaction volumes, settlement times, staff performance, and revenue
8. **Customer Self-Service** — Customers initiate transactions, upload receipts, and track status independently

### 2. Who It's For
- Bureau De Change operators looking to modernize their operations
- BDC back-office staff who need structured workflows instead of informal communication
- FX customers who want to check rates and initiate transactions without phone calls
