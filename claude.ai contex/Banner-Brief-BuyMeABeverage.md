### 2. The Problem
Content creators, freelancers, and professionals in Nigeria have no easy way to accept casual tips and gifts from supporters. Setting up payment collection is unnecessarily complex, and international platforms do not support Nigerian banks well, leaving creators without a simple tool that just works.

### 3. Key Value Props
1. **Custom Shareable Link** — Get your own buymeabeverage.com/[yourname] link to share anywhere
2. **Instant Payouts** — Funds settle directly to your Nigerian bank account with no delays
3. **Zero Complexity** — No app download, no complex setup, no technical knowledge needed

### 4. How It Works
1. Create your free profile on buymeabeverage.com
2. Get your custom shareable link
3. Share it on social media, WhatsApp, your bio, or anywhere you connect with supporters
4. Supporters buy you a beverage, and funds go straight to your bank account

## BACK SIDE

### 1. Feature Breakdown
1. **Custom Payment Links** — Every user gets a personalized, memorable URL (buymeabeverage.com/[yourname])
2. **Paystack-Powered Payments** — Secure, reliable payment processing through Nigeria's most trusted payment gateway
3. **Direct Bank Settlement** — Funds go straight to the user's Nigerian bank account, no intermediary wallets
4. **Transaction History** — Full record of every tip received, with dates, amounts, and supporter details
5. **Merchant Dashboard** — Clean overview of earnings, transaction activity, and payout history
6. **Mobile-Responsive** — Works beautifully on phones, tablets, and desktops
7. **Social Sharing** — One-tap sharing to WhatsApp, Twitter/X, Instagram, and other platforms
8. **No App Download Required** — Entirely web-based, accessible from any browser

### 2. Who It's For
- Content creators looking for a simple way to receive support from their audience
- Freelancers who want to accept tips alongside their regular work
- Professionals offering advice, mentorship, or community value
- Social media influencers monetizing their reach
- Musicians, writers, and artists accepting support from fans
