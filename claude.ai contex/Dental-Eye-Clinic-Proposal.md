## Your Practice Is Losing Patients Before They Ever Call You

Most dental and eye practices in Abuja acquire patients through word of mouth and proximity. That is a passive strategy. It means the practice grows slowly, and any month without referrals is a quiet month.

Beyond acquisition, every practice faces the same operational friction:

**Enquiries go cold:** A potential patient sends a WhatsApp message at 7pm. No one replies until morning. They have already booked elsewhere.

**Appointments are lost:** Bookings are informal. No confirmation is sent. No reminder follows. Patients forget, change their minds, or simply do not show up.

**Patients disappear:** After the appointment, silence. No follow-up. No rebooking prompt. The patient does not return unless they remember to.

The cost of this friction is not visible on a spreadsheet — but it is real. Every unanswered message is a lost consultation. Every no-show is an empty chair. Every patient who never returned is recurring revenue that walked out the door.

## What We Build

Algolog designs and deploys a fully automated patient acquisition and retention system for your practice. It runs across four channels — WhatsApp, Instagram, Google Maps, and your website — and handles three jobs:

### 01 — Lead Capture
Every enquiry across every channel is caught and responded to instantly — at any hour, on any day. No message falls through. No potential patient waits.

- Instant response to WhatsApp messages (24/7, including nights and weekends)
- Auto-replies to Instagram DMs with service information
- Google Maps enquiry handling
- Website form follow-up via WhatsApp
- Every lead captured in a central dashboard

### 02 — Lead Warming
Before a patient books, they have questions. About cost. About the procedure. About whether to trust a new practice. Our system answers these questions in a natural, conversational way — building confidence until the patient is ready to commit.

- Service and pricing information delivered on demand
- Common objections addressed automatically ("Is it painful?", "How much is a cleaning?", "Do you take walk-ins?")
- Personalised conversation based on what the patient is asking
- Practice credentials and reviews shared at the right moment

### 03 — Conversion and Retention
Once a patient is ready, the system books the appointment, sends a confirmation, and follows up with reminders. After the visit, it checks in, prompts rebooking, and collects Google reviews from satisfied patients.

- Appointment booking via WhatsApp conversation
- Confirmation sent immediately after booking
- 24-hour and morning-of reminders to reduce no-shows
- Post-visit follow-up and satisfaction check
- Rebooking prompt for follow-up appointments
- Google review request for satisfied patients
- Recall reminders for routine visits (6-month dental check, annual eye exam)
