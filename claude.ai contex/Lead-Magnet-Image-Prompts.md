## Hair Texture Reference (for consistency)

To ensure AI generators produce authentic Black African hair:
- **TWA (Teeny Weeny Afro):** Very short, tightly coiled natural hair close to the scalp
- **Afro:** Full, rounded natural hair standing out from the head
- **Locs/Dreadlocks:** Rope-like strands of matted/coiled hair
- **Braids/Box braids:** Hair sectioned and braided into individual plaits
- **Goddess locs:** Faux locs with loose curly ends
- **Afro fade:** Short sides with natural coiled hair on top
- **High puff:** Natural hair gathered into a large puff on top of the head
- **Twists/Short twists:** Two-strand twists, short length

If a generator produces straight hair or light skin, add: `dark brown skin, type 4c coily natural African hair texture` to reinforce the prompt.
