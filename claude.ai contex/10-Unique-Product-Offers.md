## 1. HOSPITALITY — GuestPilot
### "Your Property Runs Itself — Even While You Sleep"

**What it is:** An all-in-one autopilot system for short-let apartments and hotels that handles everything from the moment a guest finds your property to the moment they check out — booking, payment, access codes, guest communication, and recovery of guests who almost booked but didn't finish.

**The problem it kills:** You're running your property from your WhatsApp. Every booking is a conversation. Every check-in requires you on-site. Every no-show is money gone. Every guest who starts booking on Airbnb but switches to a competitor is invisible to you. Your evenings and weekends belong to your property, not your family.

**What makes it different:** GuestPilot doesn't just digitise your bookings — it creates an automated pipeline where guests discover you, book, pay, get their smart lock code, receive check-in instructions, get mid-stay check-ins, checkout reminders, and a review request — ALL without you touching your phone. And when a guest starts booking but doesn't finish? The system chases them with 3 automatic reminders until they complete or the room is released. You recover 15-25% of bookings that would have vanished.

**What's inside:**
- Branded booking portal — your property name, your photos, your prices, your URL
- Paystack payment integration — guests pay before arrival, no exceptions
- TTLock smart lock sync — unique access code per guest, activates on check-in, dies at checkout
- Automated guest communication — pre-arrival instructions, welcome message, mid-stay check-in, checkout reminder, review request — all automatic
- Cart abandonment recovery — 3-touch reminder sequence for incomplete bookings
- Real-time dashboard — see every room's status from your phone, anywhere in the world
- Auto-generated property website — your property has its own site, not just an Airbnb listing
- Conflict prevention — impossible to double-book
- Invoice and receipt generation — automatic for every booking
- Staff role management — receptionist sees bookings, cleaner sees turnovers, owner sees money

**The pitch:**
> "Right now, your property makes money only when you're awake, available, and actively managing it. GuestPilot makes it make money while you sleep. Guests find you, book, pay, get their access code, and let themselves in — without you doing anything. And guests who almost booked but got distracted? The system brings them back. You get your evenings back, your weekends back, and 15-25% more bookings from people who would have disappeared."

**The kicker:**
> "Every booking through your own portal instead of Airbnb saves you 15%. At 20 bookings/month × NGN 30K, that's NGN 1.08M/year in commissions you keep. The system pays for itself in year one from that alone — before you count the recovered bookings, the time saved, and the no-shows eliminated."

**The guarantee:**

**The problem it kills:** You're processing 20-30 paper vouchers a day with a calculator. Your staff send photos of vouchers to WhatsApp. You recheck every calculation manually. When a depositor says "my balance should be 5 million, not 2 million" — you have no timestamped proof. You have to be on-site because you don't trust anyone else's numbers. Your daily AP remittance takes 2-3 hours of manual calculation. And you haven't had a weekend off in months because the station doesn't run without you watching.

**What makes it different:** LedgerLock doesn't just digitise your vouchers — it creates a trust layer between you and your depositors. Every transaction is timestamped, attributed to the staff member who processed it, and immutable — no one can edit it after the fact. Your depositors get a portal where they check their own balance from their phone at 2am if they want. The "what's my balance?" calls stop. The disputes stop. The AP remittance that takes 3 hours calculates in 2 minutes. And you see everything — every station, every depositor, every naira — from your phone, from your house, from anywhere.

**What's inside:**
- Digital depositor ledger — live balances per customer, updated with every transaction
- Digital voucher system — staff input on device, auto-calculated, zero paper
- Customer self-service portal — depositors see their own balance, transactions, and statements 24/7
- Automated statements — weekly/monthly, delivered by email and WhatsApp, without you doing anything
- AP remittance calculator — one-click daily total, broken down by customer and product
- Immutable audit trail — every transaction timestamped, attributed to a staff member, cannot be edited
- Role-based access — cashier, supervisor, manager, owner each see what they should
- Multi-station dashboard — all your stations on one screen
- Discrepancy alerts — when a staff member's numbers don't add up, you know immediately
- Analytics — top depositors, purchase patterns, station performance, revenue trends

**The pitch:**
> "You're currently operating with zero audit trail. Every voucher is paper. Every balance is trust. Every dispute is your word against theirs. LedgerLock makes every transaction traceable to the second and the staff member. Your depositors stop calling — they check their own balance. Your reconciliation stops taking hours — it takes minutes. And the disputes? You pull up the timestamped record and the conversation ends in 30 seconds."

**The kicker:**
> "One disputed depositor balance can cost you NGN 2-5 million. You're carrying that risk with every depositor, every day. LedgerLock costs a fraction of one major dispute — and it prevents ALL of them."

**The guarantee:**
> "If the system ever calculates a balance incorrectly, we fix it at our cost. Every naira, every time."

**The problem it kills:** Your giving depends on who shows up on Sunday. When members travel — Easter, Christmas, summer, public holidays — giving drops 30-40%. And when a member says "I gave NGN 50,000 last month" — you have no record to verify.

The giving runs automatically. The member gets a receipt for every transaction. The church gets a dashboard showing who gave, when, how much, by category (tithe, offering, building fund, missions). Monday reconciliation goes from 3 hours to 10 minutes. And during holidays? Giving doesn't drop — because it's automatic.

**What's inside:**
- Branded giving page — your church logo, colours, and categories at pledged.app/[churchname]
- Member self-setup — they do it themselves, no admin involvement
- Recurring giving — weekly or monthly, pause/resume/cancel anytime

- Category-based giving — tithes, offerings, building fund, missions, special projects
- Admin dashboard — full analytics, member activity, giving trends, lapsed giver alerts
- One-click CSV export — for your accountant, in minutes
- Multi-branch support — all campuses, one dashboard
- Annual giving statements — generated per member for year-end

**The pitch:**

**The pitch:**
