Most African businesses have data scattered across disconnected systems. Decisions end up being made on gut feeling instead of evidence. Manual reporting takes days to compile and still misses the insights that actually matter for growth and efficiency.

1. **Unified Data View** — Connect all your business data sources in one place for a complete picture
2. **AI-Driven Insights** — Patterns, trends, and anomalies are identified automatically, not manually
3. **Custom Dashboards** — Tailored views for executives, managers, and operational teams
4. **Actionable Recommendations** — AI highlights profit opportunities and operational inefficiencies so your team can act, not just observe

### 4. How It Works
1. Connect your business data sources (finance, CRM, operations, or any structured data)
