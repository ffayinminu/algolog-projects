## How to Use This Catalogue

1. **Identify the industry** during diagnosis
2. **Identify the specific problem** — which of the 10 products addresses it?
3. **Present THAT product** as a standalone offer with the full Giant Promise → Stack → Kicker → Guarantee structure from the original offers document
4. **Upsell:** If they bite on Product 3, show them how Product 5 complements it. Build toward Product 10 (the complete suite).
5. **Customize:** Every product listed here is a template. In the meeting, use their words, their numbers, their industry terms.

**Remember Alabi:** "Nigerians would rather pay MORE when they feel they're getting a steal." Stack the value. Show the proof. Make the price feel like a fraction of what they're getting.
