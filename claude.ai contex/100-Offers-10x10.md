# 100 Irresistible Offers — 10 Per Industry
## Algolog SDS Grand Slam Offer Bank

*Based on: $100M Offers (Hormozi), How to Sell to Nigerians (Akin Alabi), Psychology of Selling (Tracy), Sell or Be Sold (Cardone)*

**How to use:** Each industry has 10 different offers. Pick the one that matches the prospect's specific pain point (discovered during Diagnosis). You don't present all 10 — you present the ONE that hits their hot button, stacked with enough value that they feel stupid saying no.

**The Alabi rule:** "Nigerians would rather pay MORE when they feel they are ripping you off than pay less when they feel you are ripping them off." Every offer must feel like the client is getting away with murder at the price you're charging.

## How to Use This Offer Bank

1. **Identify the industry** during diagnosis
2. **Identify the specific pain** — which of the 10 problems resonates most?
3. **Present THAT offer** — not all 10. The one that hits their hot button.
4. **Stack the value** — list everything included
5. **Do the kicker math** with THEIR numbers
6. **State the guarantee** before the price
7. **Close** — "Based on everything you've told me, this is exactly what you need. Can we move forward?"

If they want more → upsell to Offer 10 (the complete system). Cardone: "Second money is easier than first money."
